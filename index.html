THE CHALLENGE

Indian Railways.
13,000 trains. Zero real-time visibility.

The world's largest railway network runs on outdated systems. Passengers are left in the dark. Operations are reactive, not predictive.

01

Outdated Information Systems

NTES is outdated. Passengers find out about delays on the platform — not 30 minutes before, not via their phone. On the platform, after it's too late.

UX Failure
02

Invisible Cascade Delays

Cascade delays go undetected until it's too late to act. One delayed train triggers a domino effect — and no system catches it early enough to intervene.

Operational Blindspot
03

Manual Operations Decisions

Station masters make rerouting decisions manually, with no AI assist. Critical choices affecting thousands of passengers are made with incomplete, stale data.

Human Bottleneck
THE SOLUTION

One Platform.
Three Powerful Panels.

RailSense unifies real-time tracking, AI operations, and passenger communication into a single, seamless system.
